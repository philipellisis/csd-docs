# Bluetooth

The PinOne Bluetooth board adds wireless connectivity to your PinOne Mini, allowing you to connect your virtual pinball cabinet controller to PCs, VR headsets, and other Bluetooth-enabled devices without a USB cable. This is especially useful for VR setups such as the Meta Quest, where a cable connection to the headset is not possible.

The Bluetooth board supports both keyboard and gamepad modes, giving you full flexibility depending on the game or platform you are using.
